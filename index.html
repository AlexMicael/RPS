Rules:

In Extreme Rock Paper Scissors, you control a limited arsenal of weapons (rock, paper, scissors) against a computer opponent. To win, you must capture all of your opponent's arsenal. During each round, you select a card and the winner loses their card to the winner. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. And in case of a tie, both players receive their weapon back. This continues until one you or the opponent has no more weapons. Additionally, if the same weapon is used 3 times in a row, it breaks and becomes unusable, and if you tie 3 times in a row, you and the opponent will lose a random weapon.
